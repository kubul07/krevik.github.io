  • How to bind?

    • In console: bind "key_number" "say text"

    eg. bind "p" "say somebody : whatever"

    List of numpad key bindings:

    • Numpad 0: "KP_INS"
    • Numpad 1: "KP_END"
    • Numpad 2: "KP_DOWNARROW"
    • Numpad 3: "KP_PGDN"
    • Numpad 4: "KP_LEFTARROW"
    • Numpad 5: "KP_5"
    • Numpad 6: "KP_RIGHTARROW"
    • Numpad 7: "KP_HOME"
    • Numpad 8: "KP_UPARROW"
    • Numpad 9: "KP_PGUP"
    • Numpad DEL: "KP_DEL"
    • Numpad /: "KP_SLASH"
    • Numpad *: "KP_MULTIPLY"
    • Numpad -: "KP_MINUS"
    • Numpad +: "KP_PLUS"
